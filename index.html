Manifest

I build bridges between human imagination and machine intelligence. I believe code isn't just logic — it's a language of expression. In every algorithm, I search for aesthetics; in every system, a narrative.

My path began in engineering, where I learned to think through data. But it was through creative AI that I found my voice — blending storytelling, design, and machine learning into something meaningful. From shipping production-level software to crafting generative content daily with GenAI tools, I've shaped my work around the harmony of structure and creativity.

Generative AI is more than a tool to me — it's a new kind of camera, a new brush, a new form of thought. I find joy in researching, testing, and pushing the boundaries of models like Diffusers, LoRAs, GANs, and Transformers.

Building agentic frameworks that combine LLM-based prompt generation with cross-modal content pipelines — text-to-video, image-to-video, and text-to-image. Where it's more than automation — it's co-creation.

Little About Me

My background blends full-stack engineering, creative AI research, and 4+ years of team collaboration to build together. I specialize in developing tools that merge coding with visual expressions, with hands-on experience building interfaces.
